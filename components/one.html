About Me

Helena did the last 3 years of her university career while working as a data scientist and research analyst at GeoAngol, an experience that was exhausting and challenging in similar proportions. During her time working in a company that offers solutions to the oil and gas industry, Helena had seen firsthand how technologies, machine learning, and big data tools have been transforming how corporate decisions are made.

In 2021 she was approached by a recruiter who allowed her to unleash her skills in the e-commerce industry. CasaCon Home Center gave her an incredible opportunity. Helena was able to dive deeper into data science, machine learning and Statistics and refine her professional skills as well.
The capable leadership at CasaCon quickly identified Helena's project management and analytical skills and they gave her huge responsibilities. During Helena's watch, they closed strategic deals with 6+ companies that are now contributing more than 15% of the portfolio revenue.
